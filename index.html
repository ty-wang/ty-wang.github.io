About me

I am currently an undergraduate student in University of Michigan–Shanghai Jiao Tong University Joint Institute at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

I am a robotics zealot. My research interests lie in designing, realizing, and controlling of robots. Currently my research is focused on soft robotics.

Robotics is a multidisciplinary research area. My background is also diversified. My major is control and my research is more on mechatronics and real-world robots. I am always eager to learn more to explore the fantastic world of robotics!

I believe the future is limitless. I am always ready to embrace the challenge with my determination.
